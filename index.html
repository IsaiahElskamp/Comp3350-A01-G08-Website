01 — Vision Statement

Why GiG Exists

"GiG is a platform for contractors and businesses to easily find jobs, and for clients to quickly connect with professionals who can bid to complete tasks for them."

People often have difficulty finding reliable contractors who offer fair pricing, and contractors often face inconsistent job opportunities and trouble expanding their reach within a community. GiG bridges this gap by providing a dedicated space where work opportunities for professionals and service needs for clients can simultaneously be fulfilled.

GiG will be an improvement over current marketplace applications as it is specifically for finding jobs and task-doers, whereas other applications may only have that as a side feature. This means GiG contains a much deeper and more fleshed-out service for that specific need.

The goal is to create trust, long-term reliability, and accountability between clients and professionals within communities. Since businesses bid against each other, customers always receive the benefit of industry competitiveness.

Professionals will have quick access to local side jobs, visibility into what is most needed in the market, and the ability to make lasting client connections for future jobs.

Design Decision / Business Rules

A professional is allowed to place multiple bids on the same request. This behaviour is intentional to allow:

  • Updating pricing over time
  • Competing strategies (e.g., faster vs. cheaper bids)
  • Flexibility in negotiation-like scenarios

GiG facilitates connections between clients and professionals. The primary goal is to enable request posting, bidding, and selection of professionals.

⚠ Payment processing is intentionally out of scope. Once a client selects a professional, financial transactions are assumed to occur outside the system.

02 — Users

Intended Users

User Groups
Group 01

Clients

Anyone who needs something fixed, a task done, or general help with something they cannot complete themselves. Clients benefit from the flexibility of the bid system — competing professionals drive prices down and quality up.

Group 02

Professionals

People looking for side jobs, general contractors, or those involved in businesses. GiG gives professionals quick access to local jobs, visibility into market demand, and the ability to build lasting client relationships.

03 — Features

Major Features

General

Role Based Accounts

Users can either sign up as a Client or Professional. Each account type has different attributes, and accesses a different side of the interface for the app.

Client

Request Management

Clients can create a request for a job they want completed. They can also view all of their current and past requests, as well as delete any active requests.

Clients can accept a bid placed on a request, then complete the request once the job has been done.

General

Reviews

Clients can place reviews on pros once they complete a request. Clients can view reviews for any pro that has placed a bid on their request, and pros can view their own reviews.

Professional

Bid Management

Pros can view all active requests made by clients, and place bids on them. They can also view all bids they have made on active requests.

When placing a bid, pros can enter the cost they are willing to do the job for, and the time that works best for their schedule.

Professional

Appointments

After a pro's bid is accepted by a client, an appointment is created which contains details about the job. These appointments can be viewed by the pro, and they can see all current and past appointments.

General

Account Editing

Both professionals and clients can edit their profile information at any time.
